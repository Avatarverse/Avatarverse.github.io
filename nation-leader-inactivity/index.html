Nation Leader Inactivity

Although Avatarverse is a player-run server and we would like to encourage nation leaders to run their nation whichever way they see fit, there needs to be a set of rules to make sure nation leaders are doing the bare minimum for the server.

Any nation leader, i.e. someone with the Nation Leader role on the Avatarverse Discord, who has not been on the Minecraft server in 14 days will be subject to removal from their position. In this case, the Roleplay Council will be expected to select a replacement by plurality vote or find an alternative solution if no replacement is possible. An alternative solution would be in cases of a nation having essentially no activity, in which case the nation should be absorbed by another, with a reasoning provided by the Roleplay Council.

However, a nation leader will be able to keep their position if they fall under one or more of the following exceptions: - The nation leader has appointed a regent, the regent is active, and the nation leader will become active again before returning to their position - The nation leader’s absence is due to one or more of the following valid extenuating circumstances: - Health emergency, physical or mental - Internet/technological issues - The nation leader provides a reason for their absence that the Roleplay Council has approved by majority vote

In any case, nation leaders anticipating a period of inactivity should get into the habit of appointing a regent for the duration of their absence. Nation leaders who are not anticipating an end to their absence should step down in favor of someone who has the time to dedicate to the nation.
