Resource Pack

Avatarverse offers a resource pack that enhances the gameplay experience by adding custom items and mobs with their own models and textures, alongside some modifications to the base pack. Therefore, it is strongly recommended to use the resource pack, or else you will have a suboptimal experience.

How to get the resource pack

The resource pack should download automatically when you join the server, but if you're having trouble with slow load times, you can download the resource pack manually below and disable the automatic download for Avatarverse in your Minecraft multiplayer list.

Download the zip file below and place it in your resource pack folder.

You should be able to open that folder from the Minecraft client as well.
