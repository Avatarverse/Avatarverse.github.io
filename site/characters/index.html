Characters

Creating a character

To create a character, do /create and finish the menu that opens. You need to fill out every section, then press the egg that says “Finalize.” The lore on that egg will show you any field you’re missing.

Deleting your character

To delete your character, do /delete. There is a 3-day cooldown to deleting your character, but this cooldown can be bypassed by people with the Donator rank.

Character death

Ways to die:

Murdered

Your character can be murdered if you’re successfully hunted. Read more about hunts in the “Combat and jailing” section.

Executed

Your character can be executed using an item called the Executioner’s Axe. This is in special circumstances when the Rules of War dictate someone’s execution during a forced victory. Only admins may spawn the Executioner’s Axe.

Slain in battle

Your character can be slain in battle. If you get knocked out in battle, someone will have 30 seconds to right-click on your body and choose to land the final blow (deleting your character) or spare you. Once those 30 seconds are up, you will be free to leave the battlefield with /battle leave or quit the game. Be warned, though: if you log off before those 30 seconds are done, your character will be killed automatically.

Duel to the Death

Duels to the Death (DttDs) are a special type of duel. When you get knocked out in a DttD, the winner of the duel will have the option to land the final blow or spare you. Read more about DttDs in the "Combat and jailing" section of the wiki.
