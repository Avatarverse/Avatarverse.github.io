Farming

Farming is a crucial aspect of the game, as it allows players to grow crops and other resources that can be used for food, brewing, and other purposes. Farming on Avatarverse is more reminiscent of games like Stardew Valley than vanilla Minecraft, as it requires a little bit more effort and is a little more complex than just planting seeds and waiting for them to grow.

To start farming, you need farmland, seeds, a water source, and ideally a scarecrow to protect your crops from crows. You can buy seeds at the Farmer's Market on SupƒÅno, also known as Spawn Island. To water them, you can either irrigate them like vanilla Minecraft (by placing water next to them) or use watering cans or sprinklers you can buy from the Farmer's Market.

Crops are seasonal, meaning that they can only be grown during certain times of the year. You can check the current season by typing /calendar in the chat. The Farmer's Market also sells Greenhouse Glass, which allows you to grow crops out of season if you place them at least a few blocks above the farmland, up to 8 blocks above.

The Farmer's Market also sells other farming-related items, such as fertilizer, which can speed up the growth of your crops, and scarecrows, which protect your crops from crows. Crows can destroy your crops if they are not protected, so it is essential to have scarecrows in your farm.

You can use crops for XP farming, food, brewing, and other purposes. Some fruits are especially useful for bending power-up foods, which can enhance your bending abilities for a short period, although the recipes of these will have to be discovered through playing the game and interacting with other players.
