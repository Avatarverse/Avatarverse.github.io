Rules

Avatarverse universal rules (AUR)

These rules are applicable in any Avatarverse-related space, including this Discord server, the Minecraft servers, the forum website, and any official Avatarverse nation Discord. These rules cannot be superseded by any nation's rules/laws, or else that nation's leader may be subject to forced removal.

  1. No seriously disrespecting, harassing, or bullying players or staff, and no discriminating based on race, gender, sexuality, nationality, religion, etc.
  2. No spamming chat, and no spamming commands.
  3. No excessive caps.
  4. No evading punishments, such as bans, tempbans, and mutes, through any method, including but not limited to alternate accounts and commands.
  5. No advertising anything not related to Avatarverse in public chat. If you would like to advertise something to someone, you may do that in private messages. Sending links without the intention to advertise anything is permitted.
  6. No impersonating players or staff.
  7. No NSFW (not safe for work) chat or content.
  8. No trying to make someone feel uncomfortable, threatened, or triggered.
  9. No sharing anyone's personal information without their consent.
  10. No offensive or inappropriate usernames.

Discord server rules (DSR)

The universal rules still apply; these rules are additional to those and specific to this Discord server.

  1. Keep conversations appropriate to the channel purpose.

Minecraft server network-wide rules (MSR)

The universal rules still apply; these rules are additional to those and specific to the Minecraft servers on our network.

  1. No using any hacked client, and no attempting to hack the server or any of its players in any way.
  2. No griefing any server-owned building or area. Server-owned buildings tend to be public and related to server-wide traffic overall.
  3. No abusing glitches or bugs of any sort, and no using duplicators.
  4. No intentionally causing lag. This includes but is not limited to lag-inducing redstone contraptions and spamming laggy bending moves.

RPG server rules (RSR)

The universal and Minecraft network rules still apply; these rules are additional to those and specific to the Minecraft RPG server.

  1. No griefing unless permitted by the Rules of War. This includes the killing of pets and animals owned by players and/or on a player's property.
  2. No building structures that aren't consistent with the server theme of Avatar: The Last Airbender post-Hundred Year War. This is primarily East Asian style. If your build is hideous or too far from this theme, it will be removed.
  3. No wearing/using iron, diamond, or netherite armor. (These will soon be impossible to equip, but those who are already wearing them will need to remove them.)
  4. No holding a nation Discord hostage—if you are a nation leader and are deposed, you must give the Discord to the new leader, or you risk being banned.
  5. No using alternate Minecraft accounts unless granted permission by an admin.
  6. An adult and a minor (out-of-roleplay ages) cannot have their characters date/marry in roleplay.
  7. No combat logging.
  8. No metagaming or powergaming. Metagaming means your character uses information you have learned out of roleplay but that they do not/cannot know. Powergaming means your character’s roleplay imposes on others and decides their roleplay for them.
  9. No deleting/recreating your character to change your character’s element. The only times you can recreate your character are to reroll your subelements or return to a living character you had previously left.
