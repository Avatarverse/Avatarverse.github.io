The Roleplay Council

The Roleplay Council (RPC) is a body of notable individuals on the server who make decisions on matters of roleplay integrityâ€”the degree to which the server's roleplay is dignified. Sources of dignity include:

  • Whether roleplay conforms to the rules of the Avatar world
  • Whether roleplay is fair and enjoyable to the players

Composition

The RPC is composed of 9 members, including a leader called the Roleplay Council Head, or RPCH.

Function of the RPC Head

The RPC head is chosen by the owner and may serve as such until they leave the position or are removed. They are the leader of the council, beginning and guiding discussions, initiating votes, and organizing anything else the council needs. They are also the principal authority on roleplay on the server.

Joining the council

The RPC must always have 9 members. If one member leaves, applications will be opened to those who wish to replace them. You can apply on the RPC application thread on our discord.
