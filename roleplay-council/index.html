The Roleplay Council

The Roleplay Council (RPC) is a body of notable individuals on the server who make decisions on matters of roleplay integrity—the degree to which the server's roleplay is dignified. Sources of dignity include:

  • Whether roleplay conforms to the rules of the Avatar world
  • Whether roleplay is fair and enjoyable to the players

Composition

The RPC is composed of 9 members, including:

  1. The server owner
  2. The Roleplay Council Head
  3. 7 other members chosen by application

Function of the RPC Head

The RPC head is chosen by the owner and may serve as such until they leave the position or are removed. They are the leader of the council, beginning and guiding discussions, initiating votes, and organizing anything else the council needs. They are also the principal authority on roleplay on the server.

Joining the council

The RPC must always have 9 members. If one member leaves, applications will be opened to those who wish to replace them.

The Event Planners

Working closely with the Roleplay Council is a team known as the Event Planners who specialize in organizing and preparing in-roleplay events for the server. Because many of these events are important for driving roleplay forwards, keeping players engaged, and building and maintaining a community, they are incentivized by this team of event planners. They often work at the behest of a nation planning a global event and have the power to construct venues and make other planning decisions for whatever event they are working on.
