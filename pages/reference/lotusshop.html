Lotus Shop

What is it?

The Lotus Shop is Avatarverse's premium currency shop using "Lotus Coins" (or LC) in the main store, won from organised tournaments, or rewarded as part of the "Mentorship Program"



What does it contain?

The Lotus Shop contains custom models using a plugin called ItemsAdder. This includes furniture, decor, plushies, custom nameplates and more. There are infinitely many ways to customise your player aesthetic and experience using Lotus items, especially in our custom nameplates drawn by Naviikai in order to show off your favourite nation, element or animal!



How do I get Lotus Coins?

The most common way to obtain Lotus Coins is to purchase them on the Avatarverse store. However there are some free ways to obtain them.
- PvP tournaments
Avatarverse hosts PvP tournaments on occasion with prizes available both on the RPG server and LC. Yuans, experience or LC can all be earned for placing highly!
- The Mentorship program
When a new player joins Avatarverse, there isn't always a staff member available to help them get settled, and even with our indepth tutorial, this can lead to new players leaving before they've fully understood how to get started. That's where you come in!
After the new player joins your nation, there will be a message in chat that allows you to teleport to them and give them a hand. Rewards will be given out for assisting the player if they choose to stay, which can then be spent on all our cosmetics!
To opt in, simply select the role in the #roles channel in the main discord.



When I buy an item in the Lotus Shop, how do I access it?

When you purchase an item, it will appear in your Lotus Inventory, accessible with the command [/assets]. This is where you can access all your personal items. Furniture can be grabbed from this menu and collected, ensuring you'll never misplace them, and nameplates can be equipped.
